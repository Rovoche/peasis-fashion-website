The Atelier

How a commission is made.

Lagos & Nigeria

Local Commission

For clients in Lagos and across Nigeria, the process begins with a direct consultation — in person or via WhatsApp — to discuss your vision, occasion, timeline, and budget.

i.
Consultation Share your vision, occasion, fabric preferences, and timeline. We'll advise on what's achievable and at what range.
ii.
Measurements & Fabric We take your measurements and source or confirm fabric. For complex pieces, swatches are shared before cutting begins.
iii.
Fitting(s) One or more fittings depending on the complexity of the piece. Adjustments are made until the fit is correct.
iv.
Completion Your piece is finished, pressed, and ready for collection or delivery.
Begin via WhatsApp
International & Diaspora

Remote Commission

We work with clients across the UK, US, Europe, and beyond. The process is adapted for distance — careful, unhurried, and built around communication that keeps you close to the work even when you're far.

i.
Remote Consultation Via WhatsApp or email. We discuss your garment in full — references, occasion, deadline, and budget. Pricing for international clients is confirmed in your preferred currency at this stage.
ii.
Measurements Guide We send you a detailed self-measurement guide. Video guidance is available if needed. Accuracy here is everything — we take time with this step.
iii.
Progress Updates Photos are shared at key stages — cut, assembly, before finishing — so you can follow the work and confirm details from a distance.
iv.
Delivery Shipped internationally, insured, with tracking. We advise on timing to ensure your piece arrives ahead of your occasion.
