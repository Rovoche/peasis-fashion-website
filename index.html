The Heritage

A symphony of craft and confidence.

Founded on the principles of quiet luxury and structural elegance, PEASIS is a Lagos-based fashion house redefining contemporary occasion wear. Inspired by the majestic poise of the peacock, our garments are an extension of your most confident self.

Every silhouette is a dialogue between traditional craftsmanship and modern architectural lines, crafted with over three decades of tailoring mastery.
